About Me

I am a counselling professional, previously attached to an NGO (Mahila Vikas Kendra, Mumbai) as honorary counsellor since 2015. I have done my Masters in Counselling & Psychotherapy (M.S Couns & Psy) from Institute of Behavioral and Management Sciences, Chitoor, Andhra Pradesh. I also hold a law degree, (BLS, LLB), from Government Law College, Mumbai and I have worked in various legal firms handling Property and Society matters.

Studying human relations and the psychology behind it has been my passion. This led me to take up the masters degree and started working in the field of family and relationship counselling.

Couples Counselling

Relationship/ Marriage Counselling

  • Psychological or emotional assistance to married couples/partners
  • Helping couple to identify their issues and to seek resolutions to conflicts.

Pre-Marital Counselling

Helping the couple to
  • Focus on sharing the various areas of their lives
  • Understand the reasons for them getting married
  • Understand different traits of marriage
  • Setting goals and relieve fear and anxieties about marriage

Family Counselling

  • Resolving interpersonal conflicts within a family members or family as a unit
  • Help to develop and maintain a healthy, functional family relationships and boundaries
  • To identify emotional, psychological or behavioural issues in the family and to resolve the conflicts arising out of it
  • Improving family dynamics
  • Helping individuals with their coping skills
  • Improving communication between the family members

Individual Counselling

Face to face/ Online/ Telephonic

  • Help to explore, to understand and to reduce the psychological symptoms expressed by the client
  • Help to increase self-confidence, improve relationships, solve emotional & problems, make good decisions for emotional, intellectual, and physical well-being.
  • Help to restructure client's attitude and behavior. To encourage the client to work towards behavior change
  • Help the client find realistic and workable solutions to the problems by helping him/her to gain insight into own life and prioritiess
  • Help to realize own potentials and make best use of opportunities
